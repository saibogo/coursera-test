Shaurma with chiken

To make our shawarma, first of all we need pita bread. Lavash must be made of thin dough, 1-2 millimeters thick. You can use cheese pita bread, you can use pita bread with herbs. Both of these flavors add a certain piquancy to the dish.

We marinate the chicken with various spices, wrap it in a huge roll and put it on a metal lance. After that, fry on a vertical grill, constantly rotating our roll and cut off the fried meat that is flowing out of juice.

After that, we take a sheet of pita bread, grease it with sauces to taste. Mayonnaise with spicy herbs or tomato sauce with spices is especially pleasant. Add chopped hot meat to the prepared sheet. Sprinkle with chopped onions, herbs and other additives to taste.

Gently fold our leaf into a neat roll. We tuck the edges so that the filling does not crumble. And we put it on a contact grill for a couple of minutes so that the pita bread acquires a crisp crust, and the cheese added to the filling melts.

After that, we wrap the roll in branded packaging and give it to the client, who, looking at this action, is already salivating. It is imperative to serve drinks with shawarma, since the abundance of spices makes you thirsty.
